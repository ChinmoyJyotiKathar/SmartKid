About Us

Hello

SmartKid | A personalised adaptive platform for educational institutions SmartKid empowers education by working on its goal to build a SaaS product for bringing together all the elements of educational ecosystem i.e. Teachers, Parents, Students & Admin as an entity on a digital platform where they can overview and manage their entire educational voyage.

Encouraging regular school attendance is one of the most powerful ways you can prepare your child for success—both in school and in life. When you make school attendance a priority, you help your child get better grades, develop healthy life habits, avoid dangerous behavior and have a better chance of graduating from high school.

At SmartKid We aim to deliver attendence reports to Parents along with a comparision of attendence of other children of their Child's class so that there remains a sense of attentiveness among parents to send their child to school regularly

Although maintaining Attendence Database is the key feature of SmartKid, we are not limited by it! We provide a Result Easy To use Marks Database system where Teachers can upload their Student's Marks for different custom Exams and this result will also be displayed on the website.

Long gone are the days when Teachers notifieed students through notices hung on Notice Board. Today is the age of Internet, The age of Online! We have therefore developed an Online Notification System where A School Admin can send notifications at any given time on any given day to the students.

These are only some of the Features which You will get from Us as we are constantly conducting research and upgrading and developing new features for SmartKid!

We at SmartKid Believe our system can bring a change to the existing Education System for Better!

Rahul Sharma | CEO SmartKid
